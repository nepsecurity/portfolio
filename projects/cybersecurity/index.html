Introduction to Red Teaming

Red teaming is a form of ethical hacking that simulates real-world cyber attacks to test and improve an organization's security posture. This involves identifying vulnerabilities, exploiting them, and providing recommendations to enhance security.

This website provides hands-on red teaming projects to help you develop and refine your skills in ethical hacking and cybersecurity. Each project is designed to simulate real-world scenarios and build your expertise in red teaming.
